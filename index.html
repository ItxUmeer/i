PPM SYSTEM - DCAS

Preventive Maintenance Management System

System Purpose

This system is dedicated to Preventive Maintenance (PPM) only. It is designed to manage assets and track scheduled maintenance activities across all DCAS facilities.

Core Modules

  • Assets Register – All equipment & facility assets
  • PPM Schedule – Planned maintenance intervals
  • PPM Records – Completed maintenance history
  • Compliance Tracking – Maintenance status monitoring

User Roles

  • Admin – Full system control & configuration
  • DCAS Maintenance Planner – PPM scheduling & updates
  • Facilities Supervisor Viewer – View assigned maintenance plans
